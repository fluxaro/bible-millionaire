Game Instructions

Welcome, trivia challenger! Here are the rules of the show:

  1. You will face 15 questions of increasing scriptural difficulty.
  2. For each question, select an option and confirm your final choice.
  3. Every level earned rewards higher points. Reach 1,000,000 points to win!
  4. There are two Milestone Safe Levels:
    • Level 5 (1,000 Pts)
    • Level 10 (32,000 Pts)
    If you get a question wrong, you fall back to your last milestone. If you have not reached Level 5, you leave with 0.
  5. You have 3 Lifelines (usable once per game):
    • 50:50 - Discard two wrong options.
    • Ask the Congregation - Call on someone in the room and record their suggested answer.
    • Scripture Hint - Read a biblically inspired hint.
  6. You can choose to Walk Away and keep your accumulated points from the previous successfully answered level. This option becomes available starting at level 2.
